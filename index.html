Cessna Citation C680 Sovereign+

Beta

The latest addition to our fleet is the Cessna Citation C680 Sovereign+, featuring advanced avionics with the Garmin G5000 flightdeck. We're aiming for the most realistic private jet experience in Microsoft Flight Simulator. With detailed Systems including ACARS and CPDLC and the addition of over 170 individual electrical Circuits and respective Circuit breakers, we are confident in delivering an unparalleled simulation experience.
